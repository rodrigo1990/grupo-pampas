ABOUT PAMPAS

Pampas is a business consultancy created in 2010 by an interdisciplinary
group of professionals with strong know-how and experience in several industries
developing its activities worldwide.

WHAT DO WE DO ?

  • PAMPAS TRADING
  • CONSULTANCY
  • ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT

  • PAMPAS TRADING

    As a trading company we are specialists in export and import processes, with our headquarters offices in Buenos Aires to address the trading operations in LATAM. We also have offices in Lima, Bogotá, Miami and Moscow for the development of international markets.

    Our suppliers around the world rely on our company for the marketing and distribution of their products. We focus on sectors and industries with high production volumes such as raw materials, manufactured foods, automotive products, metals, chemicals, generic pharmaceuticals, etc.

  • CONSULTANCY

    Grupo Pampas works as a comprehensive business consultancy firm focused on medium and large companies. We re-energize and empower existing businesses as well as guiding and driving new ventures. We specialize in product strategy & portfolio, distribution, pricing strategy, and communication & promotion. We are partners in growth, from strategic thinking to implementation.

  • ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT

    We are representatives and agents of different global companies in markets around the world. We develop strategies and promote commercial actions to meet the needs of our customers, thus contributing to the achievement of corporate objectives. Grupo Pampas offers a 360 COMEX service adapting to the needs of its customers, doing everything related to international trade, from document management to logistics and collection solutions. On a commercial level, we keep track of existing accounts and customers as an international sales office, thus facilitating communication between producers / companies and customers.
